Account & Data Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your FireSphere user account and associated personal data, you can submit a deletion request. Please review the details below on how to make a request and what data will be deleted.

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an Email: Write an email to our support team at support@eltrive.com.
  2. Provide Account Info: Include the registered email address of the account you want deleted and your username.
  3. Subject Line: Please use the subject line: "FireSphere Account Deletion Request".
  4. Processing: Our system administration team will process your request and delete your account within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once complete.

What Data is Deleted?

Upon processing your deletion request, the following information will be permanently deleted from our active Firebase databases:

  • Your login credentials (email address, encrypted password).
  • Your personal profile details (full name, system role, and registration date).
  • Your device registration tokens used for push notifications.

What Data is Retained?

For regulatory compliance, organizational audit trails, and safety records, the following records are retained:

  • Equipment Inspection History: Safety checklist logs and logs of inspected fire safety equipment (fire extinguishers, sand buckets, etc.) remain archived for safety audit tracking, but they will be fully anonymized (disassociated from your deleted user account).

Questions?

If you have any questions regarding our data retention policies or this deletion process, please contact us at support@eltrive.com.
