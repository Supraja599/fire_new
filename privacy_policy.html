Welcome to FireSphere. We are fully committed to protecting your personal privacy. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, process, secure, and share your information when you use our mobile application (the "App") designed for fire safety, equipment tracking, and emergency alerts.

By using the FireSphere application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

To provide high-quality safety logging, equipment audit tracking, and real-time alerts, we access and collect specific information:

  • Location Coordinates: The App requests access to your device's precise and coarse location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION and ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION). This allows us to log the precise physical locations of safety equipment (such as fire extinguishers, sand buckets, etc.) during safety audits, inspections, and emergency incidents.
  • Account Credentials: We collect your login details (email, password, name, and/or system role) to securely authenticate your access and configure appropriate user views.
  • Notification Permission: We ask for your permission to deliver push notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) to send you real-time emergency hazard notices and inspection checklist reminders.
  • Data Synchronization: The App accesses the internet to keep local offline safety databases (SQLite and Hive) synchronized with our secure cloud servers.

2. How We Use the Information

The information we collect is strictly used to:

  • Create and manage equipment audit logs.
  • Enable geo-tagged inspection records for verify safety equipment placements.
  • Send instantaneous safety alerts and notify response teams.
  • Optimize app performance and sync offline data seamlessly.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Your privacy is important to us. We do not sell, rent, or distribute your personal or location data to any third-party advertisers. We only share information to:

  • Firebase & Infrastructure: We use Google Firebase for data hosting, user authentication, and notification delivery under Google's privacy policies.
  • Legal Compliance: We may share data to comply with valid legal processes, national regulations, or in urgent situations to protect public safety.

4. Security of Data

We use standard industry practices, including local database encryption, secure HTTPS connections, and authenticated cloud rules to keep your inspection data and account profiles safe.

5. Control Over Your Permissions

You can enable or disable location services and push notifications at any time through your mobile device’s system settings. Please note that disabling location permissions may prevent you from logging safety equipment audit locations.
